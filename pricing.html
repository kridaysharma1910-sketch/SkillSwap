Frequently asked questions
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, cancel at any time from your account settings. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
What happens to my matches if I downgrade?
Existing matches and messages are preserved. You'll return to Free plan limits for new swaps going forward.
Is there a free trial?
We offer a 7-day free trial for Pro and Creator. No credit card required to start.
How do webinars work on Creator?
Creator members can host live sessions via Jitsi, set them as free or paid, and earn from attendees who pay to join.
Are payments secure?
Payments are processed by Paddle. We never store your card details.
