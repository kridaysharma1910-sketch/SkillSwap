Frequently asked questions
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes — you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll keep access to your plan until the end of the billing period.
What happens to my matches if I downgrade? ▼
Existing matches and messages are preserved. You'll be limited to 3 active swaps going forward on the Free plan.
Is there a free trial for Pro or Creator? ▼
We offer a 7-day free trial for both Pro and Creator plans. No credit card required to start.
How do webinars work on Creator? ▼
Creator plan members can host live webinars using Jitsi, list them in the Webinars section, and invite any SkillSwap user to attend.
Are payments secure? ▼
Payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy, a trusted payment provider. We never store your card details.
