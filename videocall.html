Tips for a great session
📋

Plan your session

Agree on what to cover before the call. Send a quick message first.

⏱️

Keep it focused

30–60 minute sessions tend to work best. Split time evenly.

📝

Take notes

Jot down key takeaways during the session. Review them after.

🔗
